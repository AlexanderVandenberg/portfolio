About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Alexander Vandenberg, and I am on track to graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering and a Minor in Mathematics from Northeastern University by December 2023. Look to the banner above to access my three favorite engineering projects, resume, and contact information. Earlier this year, I joined Eemax / Rheem as a Mechanical Engineering Co-op. I was tasked with generating new concepts, increasing reliability, and decreasing manufacturing costs for electric tankless water heaters products. Over the course of 7 months, I had the chance to collaborate with engineers from the Innovation, R&D, NPI, and QA departments.

I live near the Adirondacks in Upstate New York, and my favorite hobbies are lumberjacking, hiking, and watching movies with friends.
