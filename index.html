About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Alexander Vandenberg, and I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering and Minor in Mathematics from Northeastern University in December 2023. I have 7 months of experience as a mechanical engineering co-op at Eemax/Rheem, where I generated new concepts, increased reliability, and decreased manufacturing costs for electric tankless water heaters. Since then, I spent over a year as a mechanical engineering tutor, providing support to students with homework, projects, and exam preparation in 6 engineering subjects. The banner above contains my favorite engineering projects: Automatic CNC Chip Remover, Heating Core Redesign, CFD Hot Spot Locations, and Red Tag Data Entry System.

I live in Upstate New York, and my favorite hobbies are lumberjacking, cycling, and watching movies with friends.
